TrustVault HR Handbook

Full-time employees receive twenty paid vacation days per calendar year. Vacation requests should be submitted at least two weeks before the planned start date unless there is an emergency.

Remote work is available for roles that do not require on-site access. Employees must keep company devices patched, encrypted, and protected with multi-factor authentication.

New employees must complete security awareness training within their first ten business days. Managers are responsible for confirming completion.
